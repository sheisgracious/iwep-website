About iWEP

iWEP (International Women's Empowerment Prayer Network) is a non-denominational global community of women dedicated to spiritual growth, personal development, and mutual support through prayer and fellowship.


Our mission is to create a safe space where women can come together to pray, share experiences, and empower one another to reach their full potential. We believe in the power of prayer as the Word instructs.


Therefore, confess your sins to one another and pray for one another, that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person has great power as it is working.
James 5:16


Our Services

Daily Virtual Prayers

Join us every weekday evening for our one-hour virtual prayer sessions. These powerful moments of intercession and worship have transformed countless lives.

Prison Ministry

Our prison visitation program brings hope and encouragement to incarcerated women through prayer, counseling, and practical support.

Mentorship Program

We connect women with experienced mentors for one-on-one guidance in spiritual growth, career development, and personal challenges.

Marriage Enrichment

Our marriage talks and workshops help couples build stronger relationships through biblical principles and practical tools.

Men's Empowerment

We believe in empowering men to be godly leaders through our specialized sessions and support groups.

Youth Development

Our youth programs equip the next generation with spiritual foundation and life skills to navigate modern challenges.
